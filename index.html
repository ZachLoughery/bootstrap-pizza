Savor the Flavor of New York

Welcome to Sizzle & Slice, where a slice of the Big Apple meets the heart of Skåne. With three decades of tradition and taste, we proudly bring you the authentic essence of New York-style pizza, crafted with passion and heritage. Our story began with American immigrants who set out to share the flavors of the city that never sleeps, and today, we continue to be a beloved fixture in this charming Swedish town of Eslöv. At Sizzle & Slice, it's not just pizza; it's a slice of history, culture, and pure culinary delight. Join us in this delicious journey where every bite is a taste of nostalgia and tradition.
